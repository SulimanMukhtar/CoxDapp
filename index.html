The Journey of Cix

Hidden away on the edge of civilization, on a wondrous island, inhabited a race of enchanted, spherically talented maidens known as the CIX. 6,969 CIX(s) are trapped on this island. Alongside the CIX, are the legendary COX, a noble and powerful bird-like creature, always readied to attention, ever eager to fulfill their hungry owners. The COX were highly revered and the CIX took great lengths in training and grooming their COX. The creatures were a proud display of every color, shape, and size.

Legend says the only way for them to escape the island is by the hands of web3. The time has come when the web3 rise to the challenge to free the CIX. In their gratitude, the CIX swore to destroy the enemy of web3, The Degenerates!
